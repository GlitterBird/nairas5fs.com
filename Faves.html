Favorite fruit

One of my favorite fruits are jackfruits since they taste sweet and juicy. However, it takes a lot of effort and dedication to get the inner fruit out of the hard jackfruit shell but it is totally worth it!

Favorite vegetable

I have two favorite vegetables: potatoes and plantains. Firstly, I absolutely love potatoes since they're not mushy unlike peas (which I strongly dislike). Moreover, even though I like potatoes, I don't like mini potatoes since they don't boil well and taste weird. Another reason why I like potatoes is because they make up french fries, and I love french fries! Secondly, my other favorite vegetable is a plantain. Plantains taste really good, especially with the right spices!
