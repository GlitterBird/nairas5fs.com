Delicious foods

I like many foods from cakes to pasta and much more. Here's a list of some of them:

  • Biryani which is an indian style dish. Just looking at it makes me hungry!
  • Next up: GARLIC BREAD!!!! I love it! I first tasted it in the cafeteria of my elementary school and ever since, I fell in love with it! There's also cheese sticks which are kind of similar and taste awesome with marinara sauce.
  • Ghee dosa is an Indian savory pancake which is often served in the shape of cone in hotels and in a roll when my mom makes it. My favorite place to eat it is in Anandha Bhavan which is a restaurant in South India where they even have the best gravy to go with the ghee dosa!
  • Moving on to sweet foods, one of my favorite deserts are brownies represented by the image below. There are a bunch of other desserts that I like, but listing them all here would take forever for you to read, so let me just list some of the other desserts that I like: eclairs, chocolate and coffee logs, chocolate muffins, chocolates such as hershey and dark chocolate, icecream and much more!

Un-delicious foods

  • Spinach rice
  • I really like omelets, specifically ones with chilli and onion in them(delicous!) however, I strongly dislike the yolk(the yellow part) of boiled eggs
  • I also don't like brinjal dishes
  • I don't like peas. My mom says that they're just like little green potatoes, but that's not the case for me. They're mushy and weird.
